About Us

Founded in 1992, we’ve spent over 30 years building more than just homes—we’ve built a reputation. Matt and Barb run this company the way they do everything: with hard work, honesty, and pride.

Matt and Barb come from a long line of carpenters, carrying forward a legacy of skill and dedication. Matt has trained countless apprentices who’ve gone on to build careers of their own. Barb keeps everything running behind the scenes, balancing her real estate expertise with excellent customer service.

We don’t do cookie-cutter.

We build homes with character, strength, and heart—just like the families that live in them.

Generations of craftsmanship, zero shortcuts, and a commitment to doing things the right way. That’s who we are.
